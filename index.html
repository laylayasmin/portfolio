About Me

As someone who grew up playing games and illustrating, I am fascinated with visual media and narrative, and how ideas are conveyed through images. My experience extends to branding, pitching and graphic design skills - creating logos for professional use, presenting ideas and market research, and creating roadmaps.

I've spent 5 years using digital systems for briefs, creating a strong familiarity with the Adobe, Office 365 and Google Drive packages. In addition to this, venturing into front-end web development with HTML, CSS and Java. My critical analysis skills developed while writing essays analysing data, films and media, and my experience in choreographing has aided in discovering unique approaches to problem solving and ideation. I’m enthusiastic to learn and hone my current skills in a creative environment.

Based in London, UK
